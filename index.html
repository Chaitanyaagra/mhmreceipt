About the Society

A registered welfare society, run in the open.

Max Heights Majestic Resident Welfare Society manages the upkeep, security and shared finances of our residential community. This portal is how that work stays visible to every member — who's registered, what's been collected, and what every rupee was for.

Why This Portal

Built around one idea: nothing should be "he said, she said."

🪪

One Member ID, for life

Approved once, your MHM ID stays linked to your flat's entire payment history — no re-registration, ever.

🔏

QR-sealed receipts

Every verified payment prints a receipt with a scannable seal. Anyone can confirm it's genuine, anytime.

💳

Pay your way

Cash or cheque at the office, or UPI and bank transfer from home — both are tracked the same way.

📜

Nothing changes quietly

Every approval, edit and verification is timestamped in an audit trail only the committee can see.

🌐

English or Hindi notices

Society announcements post in both languages, so nobody misses what matters.

📱

Built for one hand

Register, pay, and pull up a receipt from your phone — no app store, no download.
